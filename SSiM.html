S.S.i.M.

Clothing Co.

Micro Brand

SSiM was a small clothing brand that I began in earrly 2016 during my year off from tertiary education. The concept began as I realised that I could not find my own personal style of clothing in many of the common retail outlets in Sydney. I began by creating over 30 designs of butterfly designs that I individually printed onto different T-shirts that I gave away to friends form my area. I soon had many requests for more designs and so I decided to take this potential business seriously and see where it would end up.

The next step was to focus on what designs I would want to where that were not already represented in cpntemporary retail outlets. Mt personal style consists of a mono-chromatic colour palette and so I build upon my ability to sketch simple B/W line cartoons. I decided to create five designs, four would be printed as a small pocket design and one as a hero image on the back of plain white T-shirts. The idea of this project was to create a simple but peronalised portfolio of fashion items that could be worn by anyone.

Once I had created my designs and had the shirts made, I teamed up with some like-minded friends and we took our creations to the public. I set-up and online presence, posting advertising on social media and building an online store. We also went to a variety of markets to sell our designs, whilst also responding to an overwhelming number of direct message requests for my designs. Unfortunately, when I returned to university I did not have enough time to continue my work, but would love to re-connect with this work in the future.
