Interaction Design

Autonomous Secure Housing

TimeLine - 12 Weeks, 2018
Role - User Researcher, UX/UI Designer, Interaction Designer
Tools - Marvel, Sketch, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD, After Effects, Framer
Team - Bethany Koulyras, Benjamin Fleming, Nathan Judges

Brief

You are tasked with mapping the interactions that will be required to interface with autonomous vehicles in future cities.

Objective

The goal of this research assignment was to invesigate the major issues within the government & private system's set-up to improve the lives of domestic violence sufferers. In doing this we hoped to learn about the major pain points throughout this process and assess where we could be most affective in designing a component of the solution.

This project was created at the University of Sydney's Interaction Design Studio where it was awarded:

Best Research Design.
